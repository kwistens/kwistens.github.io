I volunteered to help design and facilitate the second iteration of d.leadership for high school students at Eastside College Preparatory. The d.leadership course is typically only taught to graduate students at the Stanford University d.school. Students coach innovation projects with corporate teams as they experiment with their leadership styles and drive human-centered design work in their partner organizations.

The students at Eastside lead corporate teams from Survey Monkey and SAP through 2-week innovation projects. I participated as a full-time volunteer for 2-weeks; I was a design thinking resource and leadership mentor for three high school students. The students I worked with led a team from Survey Monkey through the design thinking process with great success!
