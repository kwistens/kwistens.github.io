Kristen says, “Hello!”

I am a product designer originally from Los Angeles but relocated to the bay area to study Product Design at Stanford University. While there, I began work on a Master's in Computer Science but decided to take a hiatus to pursue industry projects. I've also worked as a design thinking consultant, leading a 10-week team project at Novartis Diagnostics. Now, I work as a product designer (and active sunshine seeker) in San Francisco, CA.
