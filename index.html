Kristen says, “Hello!”

I am product designer originally hailing from the greater Los Angeles area. After studying Product Design at Stanford University, I got caught up in the exciting world of design. I decided to pause work on my Master's in Computer Science to work on some industry projects. I've also worked as a design thinking consultant, and led a 10 week team project Novartis Diagnostics. Now I am working as a product designer and sunshine seeker in San Francisco, CA.
